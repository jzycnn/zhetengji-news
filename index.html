In his view, platforms are drifting toward what he called an unhealthy “product market fit.” Rather than focusing on surfacing useful long-term insights, many have centered their offerings on “short-term cryptocurrency price bets, sports betting, and other similar things that have dopamine value but not any kind of long-term fulfillment or societal information value.”  “My guess is that teams feel motivated to capitulate to these things because they bring in large revenue during a bear market where people are desperate.”Vitalik Buterin, Ethereum co-founder He suspects the shift is partly about survival.
